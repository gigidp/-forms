PRDP Innovation Integration Assessment

Welcome to the PRDP Innovation Integration Assessment. This questionnaire is designed to evaluate the integration level of various PRDP innovations within your office. Your responses will help us understand the current status and identify areas for improvement.

The objective of this assessment is to gather comprehensive data on the integration and effectiveness of PRDP innovations across different offices. This will support continuous improvement and strategic planning.

Your responses will be kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of improving PRDP initiatives. No personally identifiable information will be collected or shared.

Instructions:

  • Select Innovation: Begin by selecting the specific PRDP innovation you are evaluating from the dropdown menu.
  • Assessment: For each category, select the option that best describes your office's current level.
  • Submission: Once you have completed all sections, submit your responses using the 'Submit' button.
  • Feedback: At the end of the assessment, you have the option to provide additional feedback in the designated text box.
